Yes, Nasser Mohammed Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah was appointed as new Minister of Foreign Affairs

December 19, 2019 0 By Edward Ankney

Kuwait has recently formed a new government led by Sheik Sabah Khaled Al-Hamad Al-Sabah, Prime Minister. At the moment, Kuwait is undergoing regular reshuffles of the cabinet.

Two new ministers were appointed by the newly formed government: Ahmad Mansour Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah as deputy prime minister and defense minister. It was also reported that Ahmad Nasser Al-Mohammad Al-Sabah was appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs.

It was also revealed that there are three women to serve in the new government among the seven newly appointed ministers.

Talking to the new ministers, Kuwaiti Emir Sheik Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah encouraged them to double their efforts to boost growth and development to meet the people’s expectations.

The Kuwaiti emir issued a directive last month appointing Sheik Sabah Khaled Al-Hamad Al-Sabah as prime minister. On November 14, the previous administration resigned on allegations of waste of public money and ministerial disagreements. Kuwait’s Emir appointed to form a new government on November 19 to Sheik Sabah Al-Khalid Al-Sabah.
